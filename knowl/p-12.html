A pleasant breeze blew in from the Aegean Sea as Pythagoras air-dried after a morning swim. A young Pythagorean waited respectfully for the master to finish his morning routine before approaching. As great as Pythagoras was, he welcomed young and old, man and woman, to approach with questions. He encouraged communal thought and dialogue. After his swim, of course. And who could argue with the results? His school had unearthed the basic tools with which one could explain everything in the universe: the counting numbers.
