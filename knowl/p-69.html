An important topological property is connectedness: Is an object in one piece? No matter how you morph an object in a continuous fashion (no ripping or tearing!) a space that is connected will stay connected. In topology, connected spaces are of primary importance. Sadly, most integers have disconnected symbols, a significant strike against them. For instance, -33 has a cumbersome 3 connected components. Of the integers, only 0 through 9 are connected.
