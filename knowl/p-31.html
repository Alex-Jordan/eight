Shortly after Pythagoras enjoyed his daily swims, \(8\) made a bold and magnificent breakthrough in a great land to the east. I refer to the Noble Eightfold Path to enlightenment as taught by Gautama Buddha in eastern India. The eightfold path is as profound as it is straightforward: (1) Right view; (2) Right intention; (3) Right speech; (4) Right action; (5) Right livelihood; (6) Right effort; (7) Right meditation; (8) Right contemplation. Behold! We see a remarkable nod to the self-assured, self-sufficient beauty of the number \(8\text{,}\), with the added bonus of being a blueprint for leading a life free of suffering.
