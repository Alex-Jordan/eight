The Story of 8

Mike Hitchman
Mathematics Department
Linfield College
McMinnville, Oregon, USA
mhitchm@linfield.edu

May 29, 2017

Over the years, alert students have noticed that \(8\) seems to appear in my classes more often than chance would allow. It may be also that I have alluded to the greatness of \(8\) from time to time in these classes. So I continue to be surprised when these students ask, “What's so great about 8?” Isn't it clear?
